Chapter Nine

A Letter For You



Happy 21st Birthday, my love. ❤️ Twenty-one years ago, the world was blessed with someone extraordinary. Little did anyone know that one day, that beautiful soul would become the greatest blessing of my life. And almost five years ago, I became the luckiest man alive—because you walked into my heart and never left. Sneh... No matter how many words I write, they'll never truly be enough to describe what you mean to me. You're the calm after every storm, the reason I find strength when life feels impossible, and the first person I want to share every joy, every fear, every little moment with. Whenever life becomes overwhelming, all it takes is hearing your voice or seeing your smile, and suddenly everything feels lighter. You have this beautiful way of making my world feel safe without even trying. There are countless memories that I'll treasure forever, but one will always hold a special place in my heart. The first time I saw you wearing a saree... I still remember that moment as if it happened yesterday. Time seemed to stop. I couldn't think, I couldn't speak—I could only look at you. You were so unbelievably beautiful that the entire world around me disappeared. It was just you. And then... that kiss. It wasn't just a kiss. It became one of the most precious memories of my entire life—a memory that still makes my heart smile every single time I think about it. Thank you... Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for standing beside me through every phase of my life. When I was confused... When I was stressed... When I was broken... When I doubted myself... And even during my happiest moments... You were always there. You never gave up on me, even during the days when I wasn't the best version of myself. That kind of love is incredibly rare. And I promise I'll spend the rest of my life making sure you never have to question how deeply you're loved. I love every little thing about you. The way you laugh. The sparkle in your eyes when you're excited. The way you care for everyone around you. The cute way you get angry. The way you smile without realizing it. Even your little habits that you probably don't notice have become the reasons I fall in love with you over and over again. There isn't a single version of you that I don't love. Being far away from you has been one of the hardest journeys of my life. Some days I wish I could simply hold your hand. Some days I wish I could hug you so tightly that all the distance between us disappears. Some days I wish I could just sit beside you in complete silence because even silence feels beautiful when it's with you. But no matter how many miles separate us... Distance has never been strong enough to separate our hearts. Every sunrise brings us one day closer to the life we've always dreamed of. One day... There won't be late-night goodbyes. There won't be countdowns until the next meeting. There won't be screens between us. There will only be us. Together. Forever. Today isn't just your birthday. Today is a celebration of the person who changed my life in every possible way. And today, I want to make you a promise. I promise to choose you every single day. I promise to love you more than I did yesterday and less than I will tomorrow. I promise to protect your heart as carefully as you've protected mine. I promise to support every dream you chase. To celebrate every success with you. To stand beside you through every challenge. To make you laugh when you want to cry. To hold your hand when life becomes difficult. And to remind you every single day that you are the most beautiful part of my world. I don't know what the future has planned for us. But I know one thing with complete certainty. As long as I have you... I already have everything I've ever prayed for. You are my home. My safest place. My best friend. My biggest blessing. My favorite hello. My hardest goodbye. And the greatest love of my life. Happy 21st Birthday, my beautiful Sneh. ❤️ May this year fill your life with endless happiness, good health, success, beautiful memories, and every dream your heart has ever wished for. And I pray that one day, we'll read these words together—not through a screen, not from miles apart—but while sitting side by side, smiling at everything we've been through. Until that day comes... Keep my heart safe. Because it has belonged to you for almost five beautiful years. And it always will. No matter where life takes us... No matter how much time passes... No matter what challenges come our way... I'll keep choosing you. Again. Again. And again. I love you today. I love you tomorrow. I love you forever. And I'll keep loving you until my very last breath. Happy Birthday, my forever. ❤️🎂 — Yours, always and forever, tujha chinnu ❤️

Forever yours, Sagar ❤️
